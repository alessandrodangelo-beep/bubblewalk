What Bubble Walk is

Bubble Walk is a weekly social walk. That’s it. No networking scripts, no awkward formats, no performance. Just people walking together and talking.

1

Show up

Come as you are. On your own, with a friend, chatty or quiet — all good.

Let it be simple

What the three bubbles mean

The three dots stay from the original mark, but now they carry a clearer meaning: one for movement, one for conversation, one for what remains after the walk.

Walk

Get outside. Change pace. Reset your head by moving through the city instead of staying stuck in it.

Talk

Conversation is easier side by side. Less pressure, more flow, more real contact.

Share

Stories, thoughts, energy, perspective. The small human exchange that gives the walk weight.

How it works

We meet, we walk, we talk, and we socialise. Sometimes that is the whole thing. Sometimes, if people feel like it, we stay for a pub beer at the end.
