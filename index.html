What Bubble Walk is

A social walk, not a performance

Bubble Walk is a social walk in Bristol, South West England. It is free to join, easy to come to on your own, and built around simple conversation while walking.

No awkward format. No pressure. Just people walking and talking together. An easy route, an easy pace, and a bit of time outside with other people.

  • You can come on your own or with a friend. Both are normal.
  • The walk is designed for talking more than exercise.
  • Free to join. No membership. No formal sign-up to be part of it.
  • About an hour outside, away from traffic and screens.

Come on your own.

You do not need to know anyone before coming. That is a normal way to join Bubble Walk.

Walk Bristol together.

A simple route with space to talk side by side rather than face to face.

Stay for a drink if you want.

Some people stay after the walk, some do not. The point is to keep the whole thing light and easy.
