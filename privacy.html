Privacy

Simple and clear.

Bubble Walk only asks for the information needed to reply about the walk and, if you choose, to email you about future Bubble Walks.

What we collect

When you fill the form, Bubble Walk may collect your name, email address, your optional message, and whether you asked to hear about future Bubble Walks.

How we use it

  • To reply about the walk you signed up for.
  • To send occasional future Bubble Walk emails only if you ticked the opt-in box.
  • To keep a small private list of people who asked not to receive future Bubble Walk emails.

How the form works

The website uses Formspree to receive form submissions. This means the information you submit through the form is processed through Formspree rather than being stored on the public website itself.

Your choice

You do not need to opt in to future Bubble Walk emails in order to join a walk. That choice is optional.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in a Bubble Walk email or by visiting the unsubscribe page on this site.

How long we keep it

Bubble Walk keeps contact details only for as long as they are useful for running the walk and future updates, or until you ask to be removed.

Questions

If you want to be removed from future Bubble Walk emails, use the unsubscribe page.

This page is written to stay simple and readable. Bubble Walk is a small local social walk, and the aim is to explain plainly what happens to your information.
